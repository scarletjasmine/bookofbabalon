How I See the Scarlet Beast

In Revelation 12, we are told of a war that takes place between Satan and Michael, with Satan taking ⅓ of the 144000. I always considered this particular prophecy a recurring prophecy happening in the past, present and future, maybe because it’s outside of time and space and always currently happening.
I’ve met a few of these falling angels and I’ve been striving to organize us, though it seems like my attempts of organizing us is like organizing a bunch of cats to march in form, so instead I await on Gods direction to build us us up and bring us together. Maybe the best was to organize the one third of us is to prepare myself, now realizing how intrinsically connected I am to others. Prepare the army, by preparing myself.
I see the members of Scarlet Beast, or at least myself, as great Evil, matched with a great Good and a strong and noble heart to manage the balance of those two opposing forces. When people become Evil, we are the force that deals with them and realigns them to a more God serving purpose.
The Scarlet Beast: when you want to put out a fire extremely fast, throw gasoline on it, and we are that gasoline.
